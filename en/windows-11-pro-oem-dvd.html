Frequently asked questions

What does OEM licence mean?

OEM refers to a Windows licence intended for PC assemblers, system integrators and IT professionals.

How do I activate the licence?

Enter the product key received by email during installation from the DVD, or in Windows "Activation" settings.

Is the licence lifetime or does it expire?

It's a perpetual licence: pay once and it stays valid for life on the device it's activated on.

Can I install it on multiple PCs?

No, the OEM licence is tied to the first device it's installed and activated on.
