Frequently asked questions

What exactly do I receive after purchase?

You receive a digital delivery email with what you need to use the licence (typically a product code and/or practical instructions, plus useful purchase references, as shown in the message we send).

Delivery is digital only: keep the email and check spam/junk if nothing appears in your inbox.

How long until I receive the email with the licence?

Usually the email arrives shortly after payment is confirmed; in rare cases a few extra minutes may be needed for payment checks.

If you still have nothing within 30 minutes, check spam/junk and email Info@amlstore.it with the product purchased and the order email address so we can verify delivery.

I've never bought a digital licence: how does it work from start to finish?

Choose your product, complete checkout and payment with the options available at checkout — card, PayPal and digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay, where enabled). Processing is handled securely with Stripe.

Once payment is confirmed you receive digital delivery by email; to use the licence follow both the email and the product page on our site, where you'll find references that match that product.

The price is lower than elsewhere: is the licence genuine? Why does it cost less?

We sell digital licences described on our product pages, with clear prices and terms you can read in Terms & Conditions and Privacy before purchase.

If you want an external reference, you can read public reviews on Trustpilot. For operational questions, email Info@amlstore.it.

Where do I enter the code and how do I install the software?

Activation and installation take place on the vendor's official websites and portals, according to that product's rules.

After purchase use the instructions in your email and, for step-by-step detail, the product page on our site. If something doesn't work, email Info@amlstore.it with the product and order email.

How do I contact you about order, payment or activation issues?

Email Info@amlstore.it: it's the main channel shown in the site footer too.

Include product, order email address and a short description; for privacy, terms and refunds use the footer links (Privacy, Terms & Conditions, Returns & refunds).
