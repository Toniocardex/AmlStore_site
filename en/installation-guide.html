Installation guide

Last updated: 20 April 2026

This page provides general guidance for digital products. Each product has its own flow: always read the product page on our Site and the publisher’s official documentation (e.g. Microsoft).

Before you start

  • Check the publisher’s system requirements.
  • Back up important files before major system changes or uninstalls.
  • Have a publisher account (e.g. Microsoft account) if activation requires it.

Redeeming your key

After purchase you will receive instructions by email or in the order confirmation. Enter the key only on the publisher’s official websites or apps. Do not share your key with third parties.

Existing trials or versions

If you move from a trial to a paid licence, follow the publisher’s guide to convert or uninstall correctly to avoid activation errors.

Technical issues

If activation fails, note error messages and screenshots and email Info@amlstore.it with your order number. For issues inside the publisher’s app, their official support is often required.
