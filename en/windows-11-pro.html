FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between Windows 11 Home and Pro?

The Pro edition adds professional features like BitLocker, Hyper-V, Remote Desktop and Group Policy, designed for business or IT use. Home covers home and gaming use.

Is the licence lifetime or does it expire?

It's a perpetual licence: pay once and it stays valid for life on the device it's activated on.

What happens if my PC doesn't have a TPM 2.0 chip?

TPM 2.0 is a mandatory hardware requirement for Windows 11. Check compatibility with Microsoft's free "PC Health Check" tool before purchasing.

Can I upgrade from Windows 10 Pro to Windows 11 Pro?

Yes, if your PC meets the minimum requirements you can use the product key to activate the upgrade directly from Windows settings, without reinstalling from scratch.

Can I get an invoice?

Yes, an electronic invoice is available for businesses by entering a VAT/SDI code at checkout.
