Frequently asked questions

Short answers: always follow Microsoft’s latest documentation and the terms shown at checkout.

What is the difference between Microsoft 365 Family and Personal?

Family is designed to share the plan with your Microsoft family group (up to six people), each with their own account and OneDrive space, within Microsoft’s published limits. Personal covers a single user with 1 TB, per Microsoft’s current terms.

How do I invite other members after purchase?

After activation on your Microsoft account, use the Family sharing features in your Microsoft account/subscriptions area, as documented by Microsoft for your licence term.

Is it a subscription or a one-time purchase?

It is a subscription with annual renewal (12 months purchased on this page). Duration, renewal and renewal pricing depend on Microsoft and the information shown at checkout.

Can I use Office apps offline?

Yes: with desktop apps installed you can work offline; you still need periodic connectivity for licence checks, updates and cloud services such as OneDrive, as described by Microsoft.
